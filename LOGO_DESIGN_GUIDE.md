### Element Breakdown

#### 1. Icon/Symbol (Graduation Cap or Islamic Calligraphy)
- **Color**: Gold `#C9A227`
- **Style**: Bold, simple, recognizable
- **Size**: Large enough to be visible at small sizes
- **Suggestions**: 
  - Graduation cap with tassel
  - Islamic calligraphy symbol
  - Open Qur'an with rays
  - Arabic letter "Ù‚" (Qaf for Qur'an)

#### 2. "AKURU" Text
- **Color**: Deep Maroon `#6E1E25`
- **Font Weight**: Bold (700 or higher)
- **Font Style**: Serif (elegant, academic)
- **Recommended Fonts**:
  - Playfair Display (classic elegance)
  - Cinzel (Roman heritage feel)
  - Cormorant Garamond (sophisticated)
  - Libre Baskerville (traditional academic)
- **Letter Spacing**: Slightly wide for elegance
- **Size**: Large, dominant

#### 3. "INSTITUTE" Text
- **Color**: Deep Maroon `#6E1E25`
- **Font Weight**: Medium (500) or Regular (400)
- **Font Style**: Sans-serif (clean, modern) or lighter serif
- **Recommended Fonts**:
  - Inter (modern, clean)
  - Poppins (friendly professional)
  - Montserrat (geometric elegance)
  - Or same serif as "AKURU" but lighter weight
- **Letter Spacing**: Wide tracking for sophistication
- **Size**: ~40-50% of "AKURU" size

#### 4. Optional Elements
- **Separator Line**: Gold `#C9A227` - thin horizontal line between AKURU and INSTITUTE
- **Accent Details**: Gold highlights or decorative elements
- **Border/Frame**: Subtle gold outline (if needed)

## Logo Variations Required

### 1. Full Color Logo (Primary)
- Use on: Website, official documents, presentations
- Background: White `#FFFFFF` or Transparent
- All colors as specified above

### 2. White/Light Background Version
- Icon: Gold `#C9A227`
- Text: Deep Maroon `#6E1E25`
- Background: Transparent or White

### 3. Dark Background Version (Reversed)
- Icon: Gold `#C9A227` (brighter if needed)
- Text: White `#FFFFFF` or Light Beige `#F9F4EE`
- Background: Maroon `#6E1E25` or Dark

### 4. Maroon Background Version
- Icon: Gold `#C9A227`
- Text: Gold `#C9A227` or White `#FFFFFF`
- Background: Deep Maroon `#6E1E25`

### 5. Monochrome Version (One Color)
- Everything: Deep Maroon `#6E1E25`
- Use for: Stamps, embossing, single-color printing
- Background: Transparent

### 6. Favicon/App Icon Version
- Simplified icon only (no text)
- Gold `#C9A227` icon on Maroon `#6E1E25` background
- Or Maroon icon on Gold background
- Size: 512x512px minimum

## Usage Guidelines

### Minimum Size
- Logo should be readable at **minimum 30px height**
- On business cards: minimum 20mm width
- On website: minimum 40px height

### Clear Space
- Maintain clear space around logo = **height of "I" in INSTITUTE**
- No other elements should intrude this space

### Do's
âœ… Use on white, beige, or light backgrounds
