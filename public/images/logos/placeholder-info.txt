AKURU INSTITUTE LOGO SETUP
==========================

To use the official Akuru Institute logo:

1. SAVE THE OFFICIAL LOGO as one of these files:
   - public/images/logos/akuru-logo.png (recommended)
   - public/images/logos/akuru-logo.jpg
   - public/images/logos/akuru-logo.svg

2. RECOMMENDED SPECIFICATIONS:
   - Format: PNG with transparent background (preferred)
   - Size: 200x60px or similar aspect ratio
   - Resolution: High-DPI ready (300 DPI or higher)
   - Background: Transparent or white

3. WHERE TO FIND THE LOGO:
   - Contact Akuru Institute administration
   - Check their official website
   - Request from their marketing/communications team
   - Look for official letterhead or documents

4. CURRENT FALLBACK:
   The system currently shows a beautiful Islamic-themed fallback design
   with crescent moon, star, and Arabic/English text until the real logo
   is added.

5. AUTOMATIC DETECTION:
   Once you place the logo file, it will automatically appear throughout
   the system without any code changes needed.

LOCATIONS WHERE LOGO APPEARS:
- Public website header (all language versions)
- Admin dashboard
- Login pages
- Email templates
- PDF documents (future)
- Mobile app (future)
